LS330 spectral Illuminance Meter is a versatile instrument designed with a spectral sensor. It is used for measuring illuminance, UV index, flicker frequency, standard deviation of color matching (SDCM), color rendering index (CRI), correlated color temperature (CCT), blue light hazard ratio, temperature, and humidity in various scenarios such as lamps and lanterns lighting, outdoor lighting, greenhouse lighting, and stage lighting. The instrument also can display the spectral curve, which helps identify the type of lighting fixture.

Features

Multi-parameter, wider application

The instrument can measure illuminance, color temperature, flicker frequency, UV index, SDCM, CRI, temperature, and humidity simultaneously. It displays spectral curve for comprehensive evaluation of lighting characteristics.

High-precision spectral sensors suitable for human eyes spectrum

The instrument is designed with a spectral sensor, and the spectral response matches the V(λ) function. This ensures accurate measurement precision.

Flicker measurement, easily determine if lighting meets requirements

The instrument features a flicker measurement function, can evaluate whether lighting meets requirements based on flicker frequency and percent flicker. This provides reference for sales and R&D of lighting.

CRI and SDCM measurement

The instrument has CRI and SDCM measurement function, clearly showing the difference between the testing light source and the standard light source.

Spectral curve measurement

The instrument has spectral measurement function, allowing identification of spectral gaps and blue light protection capabilities.

High precision, pass authoritative metrology inspection

The instrument's illuminance and color temperature measurements are ensured to pass authoritativeinspection.

Rich statistical functions

Simultaneously displays real-time, maximum, minimum, and average illuminance values.

Instant refresh, response time less than 0.7s
